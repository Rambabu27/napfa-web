NAPFA’s Master Marketing Classes are curated sessions that help you build your marketing acumen and put theory into practice. The Master Marketing Classes provide hands-on learning and real-world examples to help you:

  • Capture new email addresses and names and expand your email list
  • Build drip marketing and lead nurture sequences
  • Spike online visibility and authority while fueling social media and email campaigns

Earn 3.5 NAPFA CEs for each Master Class you complete.

Cost to attend:

  • Members: Complimentary
  • Non-members: $100 for each Master Class (series of three sessions)

The Marketing Playbook Trilogy

The “Marketing Playbook Trilogy” is made of three Master Classes:

  • Master Class #1: Using Gated Lead Magnets as a "Pull Strategy"
  • Master Class #2: Using Email Marketing and Social Media as "Push Strategies"
  • Master Class #3: Using Custom Content as an Inbound Marketing "Pull Strategy"

Unlike NAPFA’s previous Marketing Playbook Webinars, these Master Classes were designed to complete as a series. Classes will be live and available on-demand (sessions 1 & 2) after the initial run date.

Each Master Class includes three sessions:

  • Session 1: Discuss “the what” and “the why” (60 min)
  • Session 2: Dig deeper with “the how” and complete an assignment that puts the theory into practice (60 min)
  • Session 3: An interactive review of participants’ implementation with real-time feedback (75 min)
